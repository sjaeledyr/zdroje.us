The Community of Brohoof

Brohoof was a community that was first created in 2011 by the previously known leader Ratty. It had it's run, then in May of 2016 the server shut down and Ponyville Square took it's place. Being a worthwhile replacement, PVS also shut down about a year after it's run, bringing the last continuation of the community, EQ3 which lasted below eight months.
